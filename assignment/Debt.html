Debt Management

Debt management is the way the rich get richer. If done right, you will be able to amass great wealth using the money of the bank or other instituitions. The following are ways to better manage your debt.

  • Before using debt, work out the investment opportunity against its risk to make sure you're making calculated risks
  • Look out for lenders who offer low interests rates and flexible payment options in case of situations where you're unable to payback
  • Don't lend more than you can manage, that is a recipe for distaser
